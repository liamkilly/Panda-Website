Food

A giant panda's appetite for bamboo is unstoppable. They eat bamboo 12 hours a day, that adds up to 12.5 kg of bamboo each day. The reason they eat so much bamboo is because it's low in nutrients. Because they have the digestive system of a carnivore giant pandas also eat rodents, fish, insects and birds. Eating both vegetation and meat makes giant pandas omnivores. Their stomach is ideal for digesting bamboo because the walls of the stomach are extra-muscular to digest the wood of the bamboo.
