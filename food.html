Food

Giant Pandas are mostly herbivores (this means that it only eats plants)

99% of panda's diet is bamboo but it does branch out (pun intended!) and eat other plants and sometimes even meat. 

Because they are a member of the bear family, giant pandas have the digestive system of a carnivore but they have evolved to digest bamboo.

Because bamboo has so little nutritional value pandas have to consume 26 to 84 pounds per day!
